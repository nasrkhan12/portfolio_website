CFD Simulation of Flow Passing Over a Circular Cylinder

In this research, we worked on OpenFOAM software to conduct a direct numerical simulation (DNS) of incompressible fluid flow around a circular cylinder within a confined plane channel..

The numerical model, utilizing the Finite Volume Method, aimed to study the behavior of the flow at different Reynolds numbers ranging from 2 to 4×10^6. The primary objectives were to analyze the drag coefficient and vortex shedding phenomena.

Additionally, the study contributes to the broader understanding of complex flow patterns around simple structures, such as circular cylinders offering valuable insights applicable to more intricate geometries and serving as a benchmark case for researchers in the field of computational fluid dynamics (CFD) analysis.
